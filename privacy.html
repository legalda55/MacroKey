Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

MacroKey is a productivity app that helps you save and paste text blocks on your Android device. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data MacroKey handles and how.

Data Storage

All text blocks you create in MacroKey are stored locally on your device using an on-device database. Your data never leaves your phone. There are no servers, no cloud storage, and no remote backups.

Data Collection

MacroKey does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

MacroKey does not use analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs of any kind.

Accessibility Service

MacroKey uses Android's Accessibility Service solely to paste text into other apps when you tap a text block from the floating panel. This service is used only to perform the paste action you initiate. It does not read, record, or transmit any content from other apps.

Overlay Permission

MacroKey uses the "Display over other apps" permission to show the floating button (M) on your screen. This overlay is used only to display your text blocks for quick access.

Purchases

MacroKey offers an optional one-time in-app purchase through Google Play. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

MacroKey does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data. The only third-party service used is Google Play Billing for processing purchases.

Children's Privacy

MacroKey is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our listing on Google Play.
